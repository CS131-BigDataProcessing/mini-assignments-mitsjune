a. What other types of tasks (provide 2) could benefit from automation using cron or at? (4 pts)

  1. automated email reminders
  2. automated syncing of files to cloud storage

b. What potential risks are there with poorly managed automated tasks, and how can you avoid them (e.g., ensuring backups work correctly)? (2 pts)

  - Tasks running too often caused by incorrectly scheduled task times.
	Solution: 
	Double check and ensure their schedules work as expected.
  - Accidentally deleting important files.
	Solution:
	Use absolute paths for target directories and log deleted files.
